Page Purpose -

The purpose behind the creation of this website was due to how the Event Calendar on Fresno State's website is unintuitive and not visually pleasing for some of F.S. students.

Instead of complaining to Administration about this eyesore, members of the Computer Science Club took it upon themselves to design and make a better-looking and intuitive version.

With that said, thank you for using our services and have a nice day.
